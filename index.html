Matt Mejia

Electrical Engineer

Welcome!

I'm Matt, an electrical engineer who loves building things that blend hardware and software. While my professional work focuses on PCB design and embedded systems, there's a lot more to my story than what you'll find on my portfolio site.

My Journey

Growing up, I was always the kid taking things apart to see how they worked (and sometimes successfully putting them back together). That curiosity led me to pursue Electrical Engineering, where I discovered my love for turning ideas into tangible, working devices.

Beyond the Workbench

When I'm not designing circuits or debugging code, you can find me exploring new technologies, tinkering with personal projects, or diving into the latest tech trends. I believe the best engineering happens when you combine technical skills with creative problem-solving and a willingness to learn from failure.

What Drives Me

I'm passionate about making technology more accessible and understanding how things work at a fundamental level. Every project is an opportunity to learn something new, whether it's mastering a new tool, understanding a complex protocol, or simply finding a more elegant solution to a problem.
